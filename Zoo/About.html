ABOUT US


KING's ZOO, a project by Anshul Rajput & Ravi Rajput was initiated in 2016. The project was initiated with a view of careful breeding and caretaking of animals and at the same time serving education purpose. The Zoo, earlier had only 1 acre of land in its holding when started. While today it has about 15.5 acre of land and proudly rears 15 different animals. We at KING's ZOO firmly believe in the "VASUDEV KUTUMBAKAM"-which says EARTH IS ONE FAMILY and we here peacefully live with some of our own family-members. The organization receives donation from the gov. and many othe big companies and records of all the Expenditures, investments and other monetary details are freely available on the zoo-office counter.
